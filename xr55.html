XR-55

Game description

Game description: 3D exploration game

Platform: VR

Engine: Unity

Language: C#

Year: 2025

Project type: Team work (5 people)

First VR project. The player arrives on an alien planet with a mission from their command: steal samples of plants and rocks. The planet is inhabited by peaceful residents, but a patrolling alien guard must be avoided. To win, the player needs to collect the required samples using a scanner while staying out of the guard’s sight.

My input

-Hand-gesture recognition (movement, scanning, UI interaction, opening the menu)

-Creating the game’s atmosphere (lighting and environment mood)

-3D model of the bridge and its textures

-Programming the menu
