About me

Introduction, resume, contacts

Maria Grigoreva

I am studying Creative Media & Game Technologies at Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen, Netherlands. I am passionate about programming and want to develop in it professionally. Sometimes I do 3D modeling and drawing, mostly as a hobby.

In 2022, I started studying game development on my own after a friend complained about the lack of interesting games on Steam and jokingly asked me to create a new game. Studying the material out of interest and having experience in programming at that time, I quickly became interested in creating games and created my first completed project in 3-4 months.

In 2024, I changed my academic direction and entered Hanze University to study game development on a deeper level. Now I am finishing my first year, have more practical experience and am confident in my chosen field.
