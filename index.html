My journey in Game Development

My passion for programming led me to explore game development independently in 2022, after a friend jokingly challenged me to create a cooperative game we could play together because we struggled to find something new. With a background in programming from my previous studies and a growing interest in game design, I quickly immersed myself in development and completed my first simple project within a few months.

In 2024, I decided to pursue game development professionally and began my formal education in the field. I am currently studying Creative Media & Game Technologies at Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen, the Netherlands. Now in my second year, I have gained solid practical experience and feel confident in my chosen career path.

Interests beyond coding

In my free time I really like playing cooperative horror games such as Dead by Daylight, 7 Days to Die, and Project Zomboid. I enjoy how horror games can create strong emotions and tension, especially in multiplayer settings, and this genre strongly influences my interest in game mechanics and atmosphere.

Beyond gaming, I enjoy 3D modeling and digital drawing as creative outlets. Beside that, I also work on handmade projects such as stylized crochet toys and jewelry. These slow, detail-oriented processes help me focus and relax.
