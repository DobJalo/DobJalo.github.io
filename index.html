About me

Introduction, resume, contacts

Mariia Grigoreva

My passion for programming led me to explore game creation independently back in 2022, after a friend jokingly challenged me to make a better game than what we could find on Steam. With a background in programming and a growing interest in design, I quickly dove into game development and completed my first project within a few months.

In 2024, I decided to pursue game development professionally and began my formal education in the field. I am currently studying Creative Media & Game Technologies at Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen, Netherlands. Now, as I complete my first year at university, I have gained solid practical experience and feel confident in my chosen career path.

I’m especially passionate about cooperative horror games — Dead by Daylight is one of my favorites. I think horror games are the most fun and leave the strongest emotions, especially when playing with friends.

Besides programming, I enjoy 3D modeling and digital drawing, mainly as creative hobbies or for my individual projects.
